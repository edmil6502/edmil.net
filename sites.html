About this Page

This page is where I put sites with useful or interesting information, friends' sites, etc. This is mostly so I can find pages that I might need again in future, but I hope you find something of interest here!

Interesting Reads

Modern Computing

  • Cory Doctorow: Journalist, member of the EFF, generally interesting ideas about technology.
  • Permacomputing: A resource that provides information on the permacomputing concept/movement.
  • Talospace: All about modern OpenPOWER systems - free/open/libre machines based on the POWER instruction set architecture (ISA)

Computer History

  • folklore.org: The development history of the original Apple Macintosh, set up by Andy Hertzfeld, a member of the original Mac team.
  • Gareth Edwards - The Crazy Ones: Articles about pioneers in the early days of personal computing.

Miscellaneous

  • Whole Earth Index: Collection of Whole Earth Catalogs and other related publications. The Whole Earth Catalogs are essentially compilations of resources ("tools") on a wide variety of topics, with a focus on self-sufficiency. Fascinating stuff!

Useful/Practical

Pages with tutorials, useful information, or future project ideas.

Electronics

  • Grant Searle: Lots of old electronics, kit computer designs, ATmega-based video terminal, etc.
  • Obsolescence Guaranteed: Info about early microcomputers (mid-70s to late 80s), CP/M, etc. Home of the PiDP-11 (and other kits).
  • PDP-2011: An FPGA recreation of the PDP-11 and its peripherals. Compatible with PiDP-11.
